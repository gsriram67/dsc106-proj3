California Climate Stress: Rising Temperatures & Vegetation Health (2012–2022)

County-level NDVI and LST anomalies from MODIS satellite data (Jun–Sep vs. 2000–2011 baseline), 58 California counties. Switch drought periods to see geographic stress shift. Brush the scatter to highlight counties on the map.

Variables
NDVI
Normalized Difference Vegetation Index
Measures vegetation greenness from satellite reflectance. Healthy plants absorb red light and strongly reflect near-infrared.
(NIR − Red) / (NIR + Red)
LST
Land Surface Temperature
Thermal emission from the ground surface — not air temperature. Shows how hot the land itself gets during summer. Reported as °C anomaly vs. baseline.
Methodology
NDVI MOD13A3 monthly composites, 1 km LST MOD11A2 8-day daytime, 1 km Season June – September (peak growing season) Anomaly Period mean minus 2000–2011 baseline mean, averaged over all land pixels per county
